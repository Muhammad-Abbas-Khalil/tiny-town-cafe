Our Story

A Little Cafe with a Big Heart

Nestled in the heart of Sebastopol, Tiny Town Cafe is your neighborhood go-to for hearty breakfast, fresh-baked pastries, and expertly crafted coffee. We believe that the best meals are made with love, local ingredients, and a dash of small-town charm.

Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee on your morning commute or settling in for a leisurely weekend brunch, our friendly team is here to make you feel right at home. Come for the food, stay for the community.
